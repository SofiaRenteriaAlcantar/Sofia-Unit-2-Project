Legality

Roe V. Wade was oficially overtunred by the supreme court on June 24. 2022, making abortion punishble by law.

Background

Roe v wade was a case in which the court brought down several laws that prevented abortion unless a doctor declared having a baby would be a threat to a womans life. The case was filed in 1970 by a woman under the fictional name Jane Roe. The lawsuit argues that the law was unconstitutional becuase it violated the first amendment, the fourth amendment, the fifth amendment , the ninth amendment , and the fourteenth amendment .
